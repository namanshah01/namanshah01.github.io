Single line billing infra for web3. CrossMeter uses stable-coin settlement rails enabling customers to reliably receive funds on preferred chain. With messy payment integrations out of the way, projects now become products. Users are no longer limited to a single chain, giving more exposure. Abstracting away hardest parts of crypto payments, we deliver a true “single-line billing infra” for stablecoin transactions. Inspired by https://useautumn.com/.
